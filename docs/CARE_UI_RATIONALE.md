# CARE UI Rationale (Premium + Intuitive)

## Why it feels premium
1. **Map as hero surface**
   - Fast trust through geographic context and immediate utility.
2. **Restrained visual language**
   - Deep teal + navy accents with calm neutral backgrounds avoids noisy startup aesthetics.
3. **Intentional hierarchy**
   - Primary actions are obvious and thumb-reachable; secondary actions are de-emphasized.
4. **Refined marker and sheet model**
   - Price-forward markers and profile sheet support rapid decision making without context switching.
5. **Subtle motion + haptics**
   - Micro feedback on marker select and booking confirmation increases perceived quality without animation overload.

## Why it supports fast trust
- Verification language and safety cues appear where decisions happen.
- Pricing and booking expectations are clear before action.
- Messages are constrained and safety-forward, including urgent-care note.

## Why booking stays frictionless
- Now/Schedule split keeps intent simple.
- Schedule options are constrained to 30-minute intervals.
- Progressive detail reveal (map -> profile -> booking) avoids form overload.
- Edge states are explicit and recoverable (retry actions, unlock conditions, re-auth path).
