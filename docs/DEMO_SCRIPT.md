# Care. Investor Demo Script (2-3 mins)

## Pre-demo
- Tap refresh icon (top-right) to reset demo state.
- Start on Home tab.

## Script
1. **Problem + positioning (10s)**
   - "Care. is an on-demand private doctor marketplace for London."

2. **Discovery (30s)**
   - Show map with doctor markers + prices.
   - Tap filters and show quick narrowing by type/distance/price.
   - Tap a marker, open doctor profile.

3. **Booking flow (45s)**
   - Tap Book.
   - Step 1: confirm appointment type.
   - Step 2: reason for visit.
   - Step 3: payment step.

4. **Payment to confirmation (30s)**
   - Tap Pay deposit.
   - Show confirmation screen: "Booking confirmed. Confirmation email sent."
   - Tap **View Booking**.

5. **Post-booking experience (30s)**
   - Show Booking status timeline + receipts section.
   - Open Messages tab and send a message.

## Close (10s)
- "This is the patient-side MVP flow; doctor-side ops and compliance controls are already scoped and in build."
