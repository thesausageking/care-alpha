# Care. Legal & Safety Draft (Starter)

## Emergency Disclaimer (In-App)
Care. is not for emergencies.
If you have severe chest pain, breathing difficulty, signs of stroke, heavy bleeding, confusion, or collapse, call 999 or go to A&E immediately.

## Cancellation & No-Show Policy (Patient Facing)
- Cancel 24+ hours before appointment: full deposit refund
- Cancel 2 to 24 hours before appointment: 50% deposit retained
- Cancel under 2 hours before appointment or no-show: deposit retained
- If doctor cancels: full refund

## Booking Consent Line
By booking, you agree to the Terms and Privacy Policy and understand that Care. is not an emergency service.

## Plain-English Safety Notice
If your symptoms may be urgent, do not wait for a private booking. Use NHS 111 or emergency services.
